Backwaters of Alleppey

One of the best ways to experience the backwaters of Alleppey is by staying in a traditional houseboat, also known as a kettuvallam. These houseboats are made by tying together planks of wood with coir ropes and are fitted with all modern amenities

  • Alleppey Kumarakom Round

    The Alleppey Kumarakom package provides a beautiful glimpse into the local village life beside the backwaters, in between the major towns with Kumarakom on the east of Vembanad lake and Aleppey to the west.
    The cruise starts at 12 Noon from Alleppey and reaches Meenapally Kayal for lunch. After a sumptuous traditional unu (kerala lunch), cruise leisurely thorough the lagoons into the serenity of the Vembanad and anchors at sunset at Rani Kayal. Reach Kumarakom the next day at 9 30 AM, and after breakfast continue your journey into Kerala.
