ALLEPPEY KUMARAKOM ROUND

Description

The Alleppey Kumarakom package provides a beautiful glimpse into the local village life beside the backwaters, in between the major towns with Kumarakom on the east of Vembanad lake and Appeppey to the west.

The cruise starts at 12 Noon from Alleppey and reaches Meenapally Kayal for lunch. After a sumptuous traditional unu (kerala lunch), cruise leisurely thorough the lagoons into the serenity of the Vembanad and anchors at sunset at Rani Kayal. Reach Kumarakom the next day at 9 30 AM, and after breakfast continue your journey into Kerala.

The cruise includes

Meenpally Kayal - Soak in the rustic village life of kuttanad, with the men working the rice fields, women washing clothes by the lake or fishing for a price catch of pearlspots (karimeen) for lunch.

Kumarakom Mangrove forests - A visit to the kumarakom bird sanctuary and a stroll near the mangroves. ( sanctuary entry charges & timings apply)
